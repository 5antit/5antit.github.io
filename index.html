Projects

Hoodfoot

Hoodfoot is a 3D platformer whose main mechanic is the use of a soccer ball to solve puzzles. This is my first university project, I worked with 3 colleagues for around 15 weeks following a system of weekly tasks that were assigned in a grid.

The Man On The Radio

The Man On The Radio is a mobile game that I am currently developing with a colleague. Here, you will put yourself in the shoes of a billionaire from a world where most of the population is poor. But we don't care about that, we just want to try new weapons and have fun.
